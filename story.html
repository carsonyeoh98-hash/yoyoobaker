Our Story

Made by Yoyo,
for you.

Hello, I'm Yoyo — founder of Yoyoo Baker. I trained as a patissier and flower art instructor in Japan, and came back to Malaysia with one mission: to share the joy of Japanese-style confectionery in a way that's accessible, halal, and genuinely healthier.

5+Years of craft

Where it started

Japan changed
everything for me.

I have been graduated on cooking and confectionery school in Japan. At the same time, I'm also an Instructor of Flower Cake and Knife Art. What I found in Japan wasn't just technique — it was a philosophy. That ingredients matter. That less sweetness means more flavour. That the way a cake looks is as important as how it tastes.

I came back to Malaysia carrying that philosophy and a question: why do Malaysian customers have to choose between beautiful and healthy?

The big idea

We replaced buttercream.

So I created my own unique recipe using Signature Bean Paste to replace the traditional fondant and buttercream. Everyone can serve our cakes — healthier and lower in calories, without sacrificing a single moment of joy.

"I wanted to make cakes that people could eat without guilt — without the heaviness, without the artificial sweetness. Bean paste gave me that. It's lighter, cleaner, and more beautiful to work with."
— Yoyo, Founder of Yoyoo Baker

情绪价値

We believe in
emotional value.

情绪价値 (qíngxù jiàzhí) — the value of feeling something. Not just eating cake. The moment the candles are blown out. The photo that gets saved. The friend who says "where did you get this?" We make cakes that create those moments.
